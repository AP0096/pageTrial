Spacing between fire extinguishers

For 6 kg cylinders ,
spacing is 5 or 8 to 10 meters ,
depending on the type and quantity of the material in the zone .

For 12 kg cylinders ,
spacing is 10 to 12 or 15 to 20 meters ,
depending on the type and quantity of the material in the zone .
